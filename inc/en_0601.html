The DND/CAF Respiratory Protection Program (RPP) protects DND employees and CAF members who work in atmospheres that can otherwise cause harmful exposures. This introductory course is meant to give you an overview of the program and why it is important.

Certification

This course is part of a multi-step training program. You will need to complete other training components to become certified. You may revisit Module 1 for details on the specific requirements for your role.

You may close this window and proceed to the Certification Exam when you are ready.

To get a certificate of completion, you must pass the Certification Exam with a score of 80% or higher.

You must provide a copy of your certificate of completion to your supervisor and to your fit tester.
